General Rules

  • The game consists of 3 thrilling rounds, each designed to challenge your skills in logic, problem-solving, and teamwork.
  • Each round is inspired by a unique theme and has increasing difficulty levels.
  • All players begin in Round 1.
  • Successfully completing a round unlocks access to the next.
  • Each round has a specific time limit.
  • Failing to complete within the time eliminates the player.
  • The last round determines the ultimate winner(s).
  • A mix of creativity, technical skill, and quick decision-making will be essential.

Red Light Green Light

In this game, players must move towards the finish line when the light is green and stop when the light is red. Any player caught moving during the red light is eliminated.

  • Players start at the designated starting line.
  • When the light turns green, players can move towards the finish line.
  • When the light turns red, players must stop immediately.
  • Any player caught moving during the red light is eliminated.
  • The game continues until all players either reach the finish line or are eliminated.

Glass Bridge

In this game, players must cross a bridge made of glass panels. Some panels are strong enough to hold a player's weight, while others will shatter under pressure.

  • Players start at one end of the bridge.
  • Each player must choose a glass panel to step on.
  • Strong panels will hold the player's weight, while weak panels will shatter.
  • Players who step on a weak panel are eliminated.
  • The game continues until all players either cross the bridge or are eliminated.

Marbles Odd Even Challenge

In this game, players must guess whether the number of marbles held by their opponent is odd or even. Correct guesses win marbles from the opponent, while incorrect guesses lose marbles to the opponent.

  • Each player starts with a set number of marbles.
  • Players take turns guessing whether the number of marbles held by their opponent is odd or even.
  • If the guess is correct, the player wins marbles from their opponent.
  • If the guess is incorrect, the player loses marbles to their opponent.
  • The game continues until one player has all the marbles or the time limit is reached.
